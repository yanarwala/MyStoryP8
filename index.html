Maddie Ziegler

Maddie Ziegler is a 15 year old dancer best known for being on the reality t.v show, Dance Moms. She was a dancer in Sias famous song Chandelier. Along with that she has been in many other movies like The Book Of Henry.


Along with that she as been on tour in Australia with Sia. At just 15 she has her own book and clothing line. She also does modeling and worker with brands like Polo.
